Day Night Sunrize

blue sky

The bright blue sky is filled with color. Different shades of blue in a perfect gradient. The sky is partially covered with a wispy white cloud.

endless fog

The fog is so thick its hard to see where land ends and sky begins. Pastel pinks and yellows blended into the blue background. The fog entices you to enter the never-ending horizon. Once you walk off you will never return.

orange sunset

The gold in the sky reflects perfectly off the green grass covering everything in a shining glow. The beauty of this scene makes me irrationally happy.

ominous trees

Something sinister is just beyond the trees. The mystery of what lurks past view quickens your heartbeat. Soon the sun will set and the only light to guide you will be the moon. All alone in these woods where no one will be able to hear your screams.
